Title: Why Running Local AI Models Is Useful for Developers

Angle:
Running local AI models helps developers learn how LLM workflows work without depending only on hosted APIs.

Key ideas:
- Local models are useful for learning and experimentation.
- They help developers test prompts, workflows, and model behavior.
- They can reduce API costs during early prototyping.
- They are helpful when testing with private or sensitive data.
- Hardware matters because small and large models behave differently.
- Hosted APIs are still better for production reliability and stronger models.
